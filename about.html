About Balik-Kultura Festival

The Balik-Kultura Festival celebrates Filipino culture, art, and heritage through a variety of immersive experiences. Join us for three days of workshops, performances, food, and fun!

Festival Details

Where: Daly City, California

When: October 3-5, 2025

Who: Open to all ages!

Our Partners

We are proud to partner with the following organizations:

  • Kapwa Kultural Center (Daly City, CA)
  • PACE at SFSU (San Francisco State University)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a festival map?
Maps will be available closer to the event date and provided on-site.

Is there a mobile app?
Yes! The Balik-Kultura mobile app will launch in September 2025.

What steps are being taken to reduce the environmental impact?
We’re implementing eco-friendly practices, such as reusable materials, sustainable workshops, and reduced waste initiatives.
