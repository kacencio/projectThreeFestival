What is the Balik-Kultura:
Back to Our Roots Festival?

Balik-Kultura is a celebration of Filipino culture. Hosted in Daly City, known by Filipinos as "Little Manila," this festival brings together the rich traditions, artistry, and community spirit of the Filipino people. From captivating cultural performances and ancestral crafts to thought-provoking panel discussions, Balik-Kultura offers an immersive experience that honors pre-colonial roots while embracing the modern Filipino identity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where is this happening?

A: At Cow Palace in Daly City, California.

Q: When is this happening?

A: October 3-5, 2025.

Q: How does Balik-Kultura give back to the community?

A: 60% of proceeds go back to the Kapwa Kultural Center in helping Filipino community and youth with physical health, mental health, and connecting back to Filipino culture.

Q: Is it for all ages?

Yes! All ages are welcome.

We are backed by the
Kapwa Kultural Center.

The Kapwa Kultural Center is the first wellness space of its kind in the county addressing health inequities of Daly City’s most underserved, vulnerable communities — especially youth and others at the intersection of Black, Indigenous, People of Color (BIPOC) and Filipinx/a/o identities. KKC serves these communities in acknowledgment of their shared struggles, providing them with innovative programming that combines leadership skills, job preparedness, entrepreneurial mentorship, ethnic studies to support cultural identity, and mental health/wellness education.” They are currently Located at 11-B San Pedro Rd. Daly City.
