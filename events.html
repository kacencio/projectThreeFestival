KORE PROGRAM
EVENTS

Day 1: Friday, October 3, 2025

Opening Ceremony Event: Cultural Dance Performance Time: 6:00 PM Location: Festival Main Stage Description: Experience the vibrant movements of Tinikling and other traditional dances. Featuring an introduction by Daly City Mayor Juslyn Manalo or former Mayor Dr. Rod Magbual.

Day 2: Saturday, October 4, 2025

Morning: Panel Discussion: Decolonizing Filipino Identity Time: 10:00 AM Location: Festival Main Stage Description: Join Professor Jun De Leon and other experts as they explore the meaning of "Kapwa" and reconnecting with pre-Colonial Filipino identity. Afternoon: Workshop: Baybayin Script Writing Time: 1:00 PM Location: Workshop Tent Description: Learn the basics of Baybayin and write your name in this ancient Filipino script. Live Demonstration: Ancestral Tattooing Time: 3:00 PM Location: Workshop Tent Description: Witness a traditional tattooing process and learn its significance in pre-Colonial Filipino society.

Day 3: Sunday, October 5, 2025

Morning: Workshop: Baybayin Script Writing (repeat session) Time: 10:00 AM Location: Workshop Tent Afternoon: Guest Speaker: Bella Poarch on Filipino-American Identity Time: 1:00 PM Location: Festival Main Stage Description: A personal reflection on heritage and identity in the Filipino-American experience. Evening: Closing Concert: Ruby Ibarra Live Performance Time: 6:00 PM Location: Festival Main Stage Description: End the weekend with music that celebrates the intersection of tradition and modern Filipino identity.
